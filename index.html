More about Me



I am Ignacio Ory, a Game Developer, currently working as Unity Console Programmer, being in charge of porting different projects to PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X|S and Nintendo Switch. As well, in my free time, I usually take part in Game Jams and similar activities.

I have a Degree in Videogame Development from Universidad Complutense de Madrid, with a strong focus on Programming, but also having several subjects around Design and Art. Within it, I have participated in different projects, including both Original and Clone developments, for University subjects or for Game Jams submissions.

Along with the creation of the different games I have participated in, I have used different Game Engines and programming languages, including Unity (with C#) and SDL2 (with C++). Apart from this, I have created a custom Engine based on Ogre3D, using C++, and used it to create 2 different titles, which you can see above.

Although the main platform I have developed for has been PC (Windows), I have also worked with multiplatform projects, including PlayStation 4 and Android. The first one has also been, with Nintendo Switch and Xbox One, the main focus of my Unity Console Developer role.
